The People's E-Book  

 

Images, Audio & Video

Uploading

To upload images, audio and video files, simply drag them from your desktop to the Assets icon. The Assets drawer will open and you can release the files to automatically upload. Or, use the "Browse" button inside the Assets drawer to search your computer.

All your uploaded images, audio and video files will then appear in your Assets drawer and can be used in any of your e-book projects simply by dragging and dropping. Use tags to organize your Assets by category or subject.

File Types

The tool accepts JPG, GIF and PNG. They can be color, black and white, have transparency, or be animated. Please note though that animation will not work on all e-reading devices.

The tool accepts MP3 for audio, and MP4 and WEBM for video. In the future, the People's E-Book will offer automated video formatting and multi-format fallbacks. But for now, we recommend using MP4 with H.264 and AAC codecs as your primary video file.

Image Sizes

Images need not be higher than screen resolution (72 ppi).

Cover images should be a minimum of 1400px wide. They can be any height, but we recommend (and the People's E-Book preview is sized to) proportions of 3:4.

Interior images needn't be more than 600-800px wide unless you expect your book to be read on larger screens, or want users to be able to zoom in on the image (which is possible in some, but not all, e-readers).

For your convenience, uploaded images over 1400px wide are automatically resized and optimized for e-book reading.

AUDIO and VIDEO:

Audio and video support in The People's E-Book is functional, but still very much in a beta phase. Please proceed accordingly.

The tool accepts MP3 for audio, and MP4 and WEBM for video.

In the future, the People's E-Book will offer automated video formatting and multi-format fallbacks. But for now, we recommend using MP4 with H.264 and AAC codecs as your primary video file.
